About Me

Hello! My name is Hamza Mohammed! I am a first generation college student studying Computer Science with a concentration in Cognitive Science at Macalester College. I am a Posse Foundation Leadership Scholar and a UNCF/3M Scholar as well.

In college, I would take classes spanning from technology to understanding how people behave. The summer following my freshman year, I would take a UX/UI Design certification with Prime Digital Academy based in Minneapolis Minnesota on a full ride. There I would learn the full design process of doing UX/UI Design and would develop a foundational understanding of conducting UX Design and UX Research. After completing the certification, I would do my first internship at the City of Saint Paul and would help their team as a UX Designer and Researcher for their Talent Equity Resources page. Upon completion, I would spend my time developing my skills as a UX Designer through my coursework at Macalester. I would use my skills in UX Design to strengthen my ability to design and develop software. I would spend my sophomore and junior year working with two of these areas and strengthening those skills together. The summer following my junior year, I would intern at Amazon as a UX Design intern.
