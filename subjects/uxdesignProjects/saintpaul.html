City of Saint Paul HR Portal

Project Overview

Led the redesign of the Human Resources portal for the City of Saint Paul, focusing on improving navigation and user engagement. The project aimed to streamline HR processes and enhance the employee experience.

UX Design Web Development User Testing Government

Key Features

  • Intuitive navigation system
  • Streamlined document submission process
  • Enhanced employee self-service features
  • Mobile-responsive design

Design Process

The redesign process involved extensive stakeholder interviews, user research, and iterative testing with city employees. The final design focused on accessibility, efficiency, and user satisfaction.
