Amazon UX Design Internship

Project Overview

During my UX Design internship at Amazon, I worked on improving the user experience for internal tools, focusing on accessibility and efficiency. The project involved redesigning interfaces to enhance usability and streamline workflows for Amazon employees.

UX Design Figma User Research Accessibility

Key Features

  • Improved accessibility compliance for internal tools
  • Streamlined workflow processes
  • Enhanced user interface design
  • Comprehensive user research and testing

Design Process

The project followed Amazon's design principles and processes, including extensive user research, iterative design, and thorough testing. Special attention was paid to accessibility standards and efficiency improvements.
